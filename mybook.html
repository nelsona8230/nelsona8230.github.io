My Favorite Book: Magnus Chase And The Gods Of Asgard Book 1

This book is about a kid named Magnus Chase that finds out his dad is a norse god and that that world exists in one day. He is then brutally killed and goes to valhalla, a heaven, kind of. He makes friends when he is at valhalla such as sam, blitzen, and hearth, as well as his hallmates. They then learn that they might be the only chance to stop doomsday. They end up killing the man who killed Magnus and tieing up the leader of doomsday.

The Main Charachters are Magnus who is a son of the norse god Frey, Sam who is a valkyre, Blitzen, who is a dwarf, and hearth, a deaf elf. Sword Of Summer
